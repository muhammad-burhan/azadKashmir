Lush Green Valleys

of Kashmir, Pakistan

People call it "Paradise on Earth". The soil which is lavished with lush green valleys in summer and snow covered mountains in winter. One of the wonders of nature which has wide spread valleys, temples, lakes, rivers, forts and high mountains. Kashmir is the conflicted land between Pakistan and India. Azad Kashmir is a part of Kashmir which is a part of Pakistan and it is self-govern under Pakistani administration.

Tourist Attractions

Banjosa Lake

An Artificial lake situated in the city of Rawalakot at the altitude of 6,499 feet from the sea level. It is a tourist resort of around 20 kilometers which is surrounded by dense forest and mountains which makes it delightful and picturesque.

Toli Pir

It is situated at the altitude of 8800 feet and is at about two hour of drive from Rawalakot. It is one of the highest mountains in the north of Rawalakot and is very scenic and relaxing. There are also remains of Old shrine on the top of the hill. There are tourist rest houses on a scenic location when you are on the way to Toli pir from Rawalakot.

Pir Chinasi

Mountain which is named after saint “Shah Hussain Bukhari”. It is the mountain top with the elevation of 9,500 feet from sea level heavily surrounded by pine and oak trees presenting majestic scenic views. The top of mountain is the camping site. In winters the whole mountain and surrounding areas is covered with snow. Due to its lush green plains and the variant weather people all around the world loves to visit this place. The road constitutes of hair pins turns which makes is dangerous and requires a good experience in driving. The road usually closed in snowy season.

Keran

“Keran” is divided by the Neelum River between Pakistan and India. People can actually see the Indian part of Keran across the Pakistani side. It is around 93 kilometer from Muzaffarabad –The capital city of Kashmir. The area is surrounded by the lush natural beauty and the Neelum flowing beside the valley gives it a charismatic and fascinating views. The whole area is maintained by the Pakistan Army which makes it safe. There are hotels and resorts in Keran which serves delicious local and Pakistani dishes.

Arang Kel

The plain which is situated at the altitude of 8,379 feet. It requires a steep trek of 3 hours from the Kel village, but the valley is worth it. Arang Kel is constitutes a wide spread wheat fields which small water falls. One of the best places to shoot milky ways and stars. The whole village is surrounded by the mountains of Kashmir and people who loves music and is very friendly. There are few resorts and hotels at Arang Kel to make your stay at night.
